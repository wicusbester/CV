    \item \textbf{Casecompare}\footnote{This project is not publicly available because of copyright reasons and as per mutual agreement with the module coordinator.} $|$ \emph{Python, SQL} \\
    Casecompare provided an easy way for students to compare the outputs of their implementations of the 2024 First Year Computer Science project, without their code ever leaving their computer.
